My name is Jacob Nie (pronounced "nye"). I am from San Jose, California, and I graduated from UC Santa Barbara in 2023 with a degree in physics. I currently live and work in San Jose while I pursue my studies at The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary.

The purpose of this website is to archive photos and trip reports from my outdoor adventures, but there are other miscellaneous materials as well. Please enjoy my site!

If you would like to contact me, jacobnie2008@gmail.com is my email.

Since, therefore, we have now been justified by his blood, much more shall we be saved by him from the wrath of God.
