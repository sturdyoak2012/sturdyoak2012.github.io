Favorite Movies

Totally just my personal preference.

I did not rate movies part of a series (with some exceptions), movies that were animated, superhero movies, or movies that I do not have a strong recollection of.

Rating scale: 4 = So good: flawless; 3.5 = I highly recommend this; 3 = this is a good movie, you should definitely watch it; 2.5 = this was average for me; 2 = this was not very good; 1 = horrible.

Some movies deserve, in my mind, lower than what I gave them, but they just have too high of a reputation.

** for movies I've watched two times, and *** for movies I've watched three times.
