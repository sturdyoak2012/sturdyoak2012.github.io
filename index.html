    • US Physics Team Training Camp - UMD College Park The top 40 scorers on the USA Physics Olympiad earn a gold medal, and the top 20 scorers get the chance to go to the annual camp held at UMD College Park. The team for the International Physics Olympiad is selected here. At the camp, we had a lot of lectures, a lot of laboratory work (half of the IPhO is an experimental portion), and a lot of examinations. I didn't understand all that much, but I had a great time learning with everybody else!

      Some additional highlights included: going to Capitol Hill and meeting Sen. Rob Portman (R-OH) and Rep. Bill Foster (D-IL)!
